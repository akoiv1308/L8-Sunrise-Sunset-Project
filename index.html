Purpose

Luis wants to build a website that helps find the sunrise and sunset times for cities in the United States.

About

Luis is a very outgoing person. He has a lot of friends who do not live in close proximity to him. For example, Luis has a friend in Los Angeles, but he lives in Boston. By using this website, Luis will be able to determine the time at friend's location and contact him/her during daytime.
